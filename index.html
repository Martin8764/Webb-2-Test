Introduction

Iceland is an island that is known to be the friendliest nation in the world. The population of Iceland is around 400 thousand people and has a population density of 3,29 per km2, so if you like to spend your time alone Iceland is the best place to go.


There are tons of reasons to visit Iceland, one of them is that you can go to geothermal lakes. The lakes are very warm and rich in minerals which are healthy for the human body. A famous geothermal lake is the Blue Lagoon which is considered the no.1 must visit places in Iceland. In the winter there are northern lights which are spectacular lights in different colors in the sky. There are also many geysers in Iceland where you can see water shooting up from the ground.
